Barcode

It is a data capture technology and what the barcode requires is as follows.

Eliminates The Possibility Of Human Error

A barcode scan is fast and reliable, and takes less time than entering data manually which reduces the chance of human error.

Provides Better and Accurate Data On Products

Barcodes are scanned to obtain reliable and accurate product information whenever needed.

Saves Cost

Barcodes save a lot of money and time as they are inexpensive to design and print and can be customised in a variety of finishes.

Facilitates Tracking And Tracing Of Products

Barcode facilitates tracking and tracing of products in a supply chain.
